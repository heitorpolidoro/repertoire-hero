# Repertoire Hero - SDS (Software Development Specification)

## 🎯 Product Vision
**Repertoire Hero** is a tool for musicians and bands to efficiently manage their repertoires, allowing them to track learning progress and quickly access song information during rehearsals and performances.

### Core Problems
- Fragmented organization of chords and lyrics.
- Lack of clarity on which songs are "gig-ready".
- Difficulty finding songs quickly on mobile devices.

### Success Metrics
- Reduction in time spent finding a specific song.
- Clear visualization of repertoire learning progress.
